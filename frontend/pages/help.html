Getting Started

FindIt UIU is the official university platform for reporting and recovering lost items securely within the campus community.

Any current student or staff member with a valid @uiu.ac.bd email address can register and use the platform.

You can view basic details without an account, but you must be logged in to claim items, message users, or post reports.

Yes, FindIt UIU is entirely free for all university members.

Posting Items

Click 'Post Lost Item' from the dashboard, fill out the form with descriptive details, and submit. Admin will review and approve it.

Admins review reports during working hours (Sun-Thu, 9AM-5PM). Most reports are approved within a few hours.

You cannot edit a report after it is approved. If you need to make changes, please contact the admin.

Be specific: include colors, brands, unique marks, and the approximate location/time it was lost or found.

Making Claims

Navigate to the found item's page, click 'Claim Item', and provide proof of ownership. Wait for the finder to accept.

Passwords, lock screens, serial numbers, matching receipts, or highly specific details not visible in the public photo.

When an item is returned, the finder can click 'Mark as Resolved' on the item details page.

Messages & Notifications

Once a claim is initiated, a secure messaging channel opens between the finder and the claimant.

Yes, you will receive emails for new messages, claim updates, and admin approvals.

Account & Profile

No, your university ID is tied to your account permanently. Contact admin if a mistake was made.

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login screen to receive a secure reset link.
