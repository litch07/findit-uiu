What's New in FindIt UIU

The complete UIU Lost & Found portal — built for students, by students. Here's everything that's live.

📋
Core

Lost & Found Item Reporting

Post detailed lost or found reports with title, description, category, location, date, color, and photo uploads. All posts go through admin review before going live.

📤 Post Lost 📦 Post Found 🖼️ Photo Upload 🏷️ Tags
🔍
Discovery

Smart Browse & Search

Filter by type, category, status, and date range. Live search with instant results and a clear "No items found" empty state to guide you back on track.

⚡ Live Search 📅 Date Filters 🗂️ Categories
🤝
Claims

Claim & Handover System

Submit claim requests with relationship type and proof. Item owners can Accept or Reject claims directly from their dashboard, triggering email notifications and a private chat.

✅ Accept Claims ❌ Reject Claims 📧 Email Alerts
💬
Messaging

Real-Time Private Messaging

Thread-based conversations between item poster and claimer. Send text and images, view read receipts, and conversations auto-close when an item is resolved.

📷 Image Sharing 🔒 Private Threads 🚫 Auto-Close
🔔
Notifications

Smart Notification Centre

Get notified for claim submissions, approvals, rejections, and item status changes. Filter by category and mark all as read in one click.

🔴 Unread Badge 🗂️ Filter Tabs ✔️ Mark All Read
🛡️
Admin

Full Admin Panel

Approve or reject item reports, moderate claims, manage user accounts, ban abusive users, review scam reports, view activity logs, and see analytics charts.

📊 Charts 🚨 Scam Reports 📝 Activity Logs 🔨 User Banning
👤
Profile

Student Profile & Settings

Upload a profile picture, update your bio, change your password, and manage notification preferences — all from one clean settings panel.

🖼️ Avatar Upload 🔐 Password Change ⚙️ Preferences
📧
Security

Email Verification & Password Reset

All accounts require email verification on signup. Password reset links are time-limited to 60 minutes and sent securely to the registered UIU email address.

✉️ Email Verify 🔑 Reset Link ⏱️ 60-min Expiry
